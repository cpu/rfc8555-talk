Has it worked? Our stats say "Yes"!

Since launch our growth has been tremendous.

We've issued certificates for over 160 million fully qualified domain names since launch.

We've also seen a global increase in HTTPS traffic. At the time Let's Encrypt launched Firefox's telemetry was reporting ~39.5% of all page loads were over HTTPs. Today that number is 78% and rising.

When I joined Let's Encrypt Boulder was issuing about 24,000 certificates a day. Today Boulder issues over 1,000,000 certificates per day (with a peak of 1.6 million).

Aproximately 92% of Let's Encrypt certificates are issued to domains that didn't have certificates before

We've made a lot of progress but there is still a lot of work to do. Our mission to get the web to 100% encryption is not accomplished yet.
